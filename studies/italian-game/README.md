## Overview

This study covers the Italian Game arising after **1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nc6 3.Bc4**. It is one of the oldest openings in chess, aiming for rapid development and pressure on the f7 square.

## Lines Covered

1. **Giuoco Piano** — 3...Bc5 4.c3 Nf6 5.d4
2. **Two Knights Defense** — 3...Nf6 4.d3 Bc5 5.O-O
3. **Hungarian Defense** — 3...Be7 4.d4 d6 5.Nc3

## Why Study This?

The Italian Game is a great opening for beginners and intermediate players. It leads to open, tactical positions where knowing the key ideas gives you a practical advantage.
